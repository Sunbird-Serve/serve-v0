What is Sunbird Serve?

The platform offers an opportunity to introduce a new paradigm of Volunteer to Volunteer interactions, followed by multiple actors coming together and leading to a societal network of volunteering systems.

Serve Beta platform can enable efficient volunteer interactions that add significant value to society and overall human development. It enables relevant actors to crowdsource volunteers for their needs and participate in interactions towards realization of the value. It provides Reference Solution, Volunteering Registries, and Volunteer Service to enable Request, Assignment, Nominations and Management of Needs & Deliverables.

ENABLE TECH FOR VOLUNTEERING.

You can adopt serve platform through our MIT licensed open source repository or can register as a partner in serve platform.

Value of Capabilities

Effective Volunteer Management and Efficient Volunteer Engagement - Volunteers are registered, Onboarded and Engaged

Need/Demand is Identified, Raised and Managed

Need/Demands raised can be discovered by volunteers and can nominate themselves for the need.

Need Deliverables are Defined, Delivered and Tracked

Real Time Tracking - Various dashboards are offered to track the data real time.

Who can Register?

Volunteer: An actor who participates to volunteer by nominating and fulfilling the demand.

vAdmin: An actor who owns, decides and manages a set of Volunteers and vCoordinators.

vCoordinator: An actor who manages the Volunteer’s life cycle.

nAdmin: An actor who owns, decides and manages a set of Needs and nCoordinators.

nCoordinator: An actor who publishes the Need, enables the Discovery and Delivery by the Volunteer.
