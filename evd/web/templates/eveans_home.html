eVean (eVidyaloka education for all network) Foundation

eVean (eVidyaloka education for all network) Foundation is a not for profit organisation, a Section 8 company under Companies Act, 2013. A social enterprise evolved from the model of eVidyaloka towards transforming the educational landscape of rural India. With a national shortage of 1.2 million teachers, the remote online learning-teaching model is extended as a platform, empowering the stakeholders directly, through a viable social business model, as a digital public good and there by creaing a sustainable impact at scale, reaching the 230 million children of public education system.

History : Incorporated in the year 2019, eVean Foundation is formed by founding board members - Vishwanathan T, Ramkumar V and Venkat Sriraman.

The Online Teaching-Learning Platform

This platform is a comprehensive ecosystem for managing educational partnerships. It includes a Volunteer Management System for managing volunteers and streamlines the process of onboarding, a Content Management System for accessing educational resources, and a Learning Management System for live and facilitated learning opportunities. This platform provides a flexible and scalable framework for delivering effective education, with a focus on live and recorded teaching, content management, and learning management.

SERVE

The Online Teaching-Learning platform is now available as an open-source platform known as SERVE under the MIT license. This platform provides a unique opportunity to create a new paradigm of volunteer-to-volunteer interactions, leading to the development of a societal network of volunteering systems.

The SERVE platform offers a streamlined approach to volunteer interactions that can significantly benefit society and support human development. This platform enables various stakeholders to crowdsource volunteers for their specific needs and participate in interactions to achieve these goals. The SERVE platform provides a range of features such as Reference Solution, Volunteering Registries, and Volunteer Services to enable Request, Assignment, Nominations, and Management of Needs and Deliverables. With these capabilities, the platform helps manage and track volunteer engagement in a more efficient and effective manner.
